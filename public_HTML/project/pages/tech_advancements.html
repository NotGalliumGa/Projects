How The Experience Has Been Altered

With the further inventions of new technology like: 3d rendering, VR, better processors, hyper graphics, and OLED The Gaming experience has never been better before. The immersion into the virtual world has made itself look more and more like our real world every day.

The Good That It Has Done For Us

With these new technologies, video games are no longer just for the casual user. They can be found assisting police in training, helping scientist study animal behavior, or even help racecar drivers get better with racing. This could mean many things, but what seems to be the most likely outcome is more experience coming in the form of safe alternatives.
