The Start

To keep things sweet and short, the developement of videogames was originally started by computer scientists working on simple projects. One of the first ever video games being Space War, released in 1962 on redumentary equipment compared to now. Eventually as the idea of video games was passed around, some heavy hitters were released. Those being Pong and Computer Space. Starting what seems like a rat race for more fun and detailed games.

As Technology Got Better

Eventually with the invention of better processors came real time 3D polygonal graphic rendering, opening the Videogame scene to endless posibilities. This is where we start to some of the big companies like Sega, Nintendo, and Playstation start to pump out even better games on their new consoles. Those being the playstation, Nintendo64, and Sega Master System. These consoles slowly but surely start to skew our percieved reality with fantasy.
